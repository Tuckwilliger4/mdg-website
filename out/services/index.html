Services

At McKim Design Group, we believe the process of design is similar for all types of buildings and construction. It includes identifying the owner's program (needs, function, budget, and schedule), creating a plan and design that meet those needs and functions, confirming the solution is in budget, and preparing documents to allow the project to be constructed within the budget and timeframe identified in the program.

  • Strategic Planning
  • Feasibility Studies
  • Access and Safety Assessments
  • Cost Estimating and Budgeting
  • Programming and Master Planning
  • Campus and Facilities Needs Assessments
  • Program Development and Management
  • Specification Writing
  • Furniture and Equipment Layouts
  • 3D Modeling and Solution Rendering
  • Interior Planning and Design Solutions
  • Construction Documents and Detailing
  • Problem Solving and Building Organization
  • Bidding Documents and Design-Build Solutions
  • Contract Administration
  • Project Updates/Board Reports
  • Construction Management
  • Project Closeout
  • Warranty follow-up
  • Post Occupancy Surveys
  • Utilities Services, CATV
  • City Planning/Building/Public Works Departments
  • Department of Education, State and Local Fire Marshal
  • State Architect (DSA), Office of Public School Construction (OPSC)
  • Construction Management
  • Educational Technology Consulting
  • Cost Estimating and Value Engineering
